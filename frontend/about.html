About Us

Our Mission

At Booster Bookstore, we believe every great story deserves to take flight. Our mission is to help you discover books that inspire imagination, fuel curiosity, and expand your world with knowledge and literature. We do this by providing our readers personalized recommendations while still bringing in books from both classic and new authors.

Our Values

"Passion for Storytelling" - Every book has its own direction towards its goal.

"Inclusivity and Diversity" - We provide a variety of authors to ensure our readers can find stories they relate to.

"Curiosity" - We believe every great book begins with a question. Booster Bookstore encourages curiosity by curating our titles that inspire exploration and provide personalized recommendations with AI to discover a new journey to indulge on.
